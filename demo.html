Lotería Demo

This is a simple test to verify our webapp files are working correctly.

The main webapp is available at index.html

How to Test:

  1. Open index.html in a web browser
  2. Click "Upload Cards" to add your photos
  3. Upload at least 16 images to create a full game
  4. Click "New Game" to generate a 4×4 tabla
  5. Click "Draw Card" to start playing
  6. Mark cards on your tabla as they are drawn
  7. Get 4 in a row to win!

Features:

  • Drag & drop photo upload
  • Custom card names
  • 4×4 game board generation
  • Win detection (rows, columns, diagonals)
  • Deck management
  • Local storage persistence
